Welcome!

Goal: Find your way home through the maze!
Hint: You may need to find a key first.

Controls:
- X-ray vision: Right mouse button (no gamepad control)
- Zoom: Scroll wheel (or right analog stick)
- Interaction: Space bar (or 'A' button)
- Movement: WASD (or left analog stick)
- Exit: Escape (or 'Y' button)
- Run: Left shift (or left trigger)
